🏰 Day 1 – Disneyland Park (Classic Disney Magic)

⏰ Tomorrow

Arrive early before the opening to avoid crowds.

Start at Fantasyland:

  • 🧚‍♀️ Peter Pan's Flight
  • 🌍 "It's a Small World"
  • 🐰 Alice's Curious Labyrinth
  • 🐭 Meet Mickey & Minnie at the Meet Mickey Mouse Theater
☕ Breakfast at Cable Car Bake Shop (croissant o café)

🕛 Noon

Head to Adventureland and Frontierland:

  • 🏴‍☠️ Pirates of the Caribbean
  • ⛰️ Big Thunder Mountain (usar Disney Premier Access si es posible)
  • 🏛️ Indiana Jones and the Temple of Peril
🍽️ lunch at Captain Jack's Restaurant (dentro de Pirates of the Caribbean)

🌇 Afternoon

Explore Discoveryland:

  • ⭐ Star Wars: Hyperspace Mountain
  • 🚀 Buzz Lightyear Laser Blast
  • 🏰 Walking through Sleeping Beauty Castle (La Galerie de la Belle au Bois Dormant)
🧇 Snack: try a Mickey waffle or a Nutella crêpe

🌙 Night

  • 👸 Dinner at Auberge de Cendrillon (dinner with princesses - reservation required)
  • 🎭 Disney Stars on Parade (in the afternoon)
  • 🎆 Grand finale with Disney Dreams Nighttime Show and fireworks

🎬 Day 2 – Walt Disney Studios Park (Movie Magic & Thrills)

⏰ tomorrow

Start with the most popular attractions (queues form quickly).:

  • 🐀 Ratatouille: The Adventure
  • 🕷️ Spider-Man W.E.B. Adventure
  • 🐢 Crush's Coaster (¡ir aquí primero!)
🥐snack en Toon Studio food stalls

🕛 Noon

Visitar Avengers Campus:

  • 🦾 Flight Force (Iron Man themed roller coaster)
  • 🦸‍♂️ Meet Marvel heroes
🍽️ Lunch at PYM Kitchen (giant portions, buffet style)

🌇 Afternoon

  • ❄️ Shows en Animation Celebration – Frozen o personajes Disney
  • 🐕 Slinky Dog Zigzag Spin (perfect for younger guests)
  • 🎬 Walt Disney Studios Tour o sesiones de fotos con personajes

🌙 Night

  • 🍽️ Light dinner/snack en En Coulisse Restaurant
  • 🎆 Optional: re-enter a Disneyland Park para el show of fireworks

🎠 Day 3 – Relaxed Exploration & Favorites

⏰ tomorrow

  • 🧇 Breakfast with characters at Plaza Gardens Restaurant
  • 🎢 Revisiting favorite attractions (usar Premier Access sabiamente)
  • 🛍️ Shopping in Main Street U.S.A.

🕛 Noon

  • 🍝 Lunch at Bella Notte (Italiano) o Cowboy Cookout Barbecue
  • 🎭 Watch a live show (check app for schedules) – ej. Lion King o Disney Junior Live!)

🌇 Afternoon

  • 🏝️ Relaxing at Adventure Isle or taking the Disneyland Railroad
  • 🎁 Collect souvenirs (ears, mugs, t-shirts)

🌙 Night

  • 🥩 Optional dinner at The Steakhouse (Disney Village)
  • 🎆 Enjoying the last Disney Dreams Show or take beautiful night pictures of the castle

🎢 Professional Tips for Visiting Disneyland París

📱 Official App

Use the Disneyland Paris app for wait times, maps, show times and reservations.

⚡ Premier Access

Purchase Disney Premier Access for shorter lines at popular attractions.

🏨 Hotels Disney

Stay at a Disney hotel for early access to the park (Extra Magic Time).

👟 Preparation

Bring comfortable shoes, a rain jacket (Paris weather can change quickly), and snacks.
